about

GGP Lucknow was established in the year of 1963 with three diploma courses, situated at Guru Govind Singh Marg, Charbagh, Lucknow in the capital of Uttar Pradesh. At the time of its start this was the only polytechnic it the state for imparting technical education to women and to develop their personality. The existing Government Girls Polytechnic - Lucknow was constructed under World Bank scheme, the institute being shifted from Guru Govind Singh Marg, Charbagh to the present Faizabad Road location, in the year of 1996 with the acquired land area of approximately 26.32 acres.

Campus

GGP Lucknow has 95428.15 square meters of constructed floor area for 13 class rooms, 08 drawing halls, 16 laboratories,1 mechanical workshop, library, administrative building for student amenities.

Hostel

GGP Lucknow has three hostels having 04 seated rooms accommodation for 410 students with wooden cot, study table and chair and individual cupboard.
